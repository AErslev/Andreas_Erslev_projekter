Solsystemet

A map made by Stregkoden, in collaboration with Mester Skunk. The server where originally made for Mester Skunks community WNFA - We Never Fight Alone. When Mester skunk decided to go alone, cause of his co-owners not helping with the community, he opened his own server. He has, over time, optimated the server, with varius plugins and other moderations to how the map is played. The map is full of seacrets, so one has to get familiar with how the map works, before becoming the master of the server. Everyone can play. So come and try it out your new favorite server, with us.

Astronomi

Denne hjemmeside handler om solsystemet og Exoplaneter. Du kan her finde fakta om diverse planeter, Solen, systemets dannelse og hvilke objekter der ellers findes i systemet. Yderligere kan du finde fakta om diverse typer af exoplaneter, hvorfor vi leder efter dem og hvilke metoder vi bruger til at detektere dem. God fornøjelse.
