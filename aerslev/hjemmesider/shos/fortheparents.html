What is VectureGaming and VectureHosting.com?

VectureGaming is a gaming community, that aims to make a fun enviorment for everyone, no matter gender, background, nationality and age. We Have servers in the games Counter-Strike: Source and Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, which are FPS (First Person Shooter) games. The servers are filled with mods, to make it more fun. Further more, we have alot of admins, making sure your child feels safe on the server.

VectureHosting.com is a game server host, which is the host and owner of the gaming community VectureGaming. VectureGaming is a community, meant to show what VectureHosting can do. The servers ended up being popular, and we therefore created a gaming community ou of it.


My child wants to become a Admin / VIP / Super-Admin. How does my chil get it, and is this safe?

Admin can be bought from the shop. Admin is bought trough paypal.com, and is therefore very safe. it can be bought with a paypal account, or simply using paypal to pay by different types of cards.

Admin is established manually, and it is therefore important to notify your child, that he / she has to notify the admins on the server. The admin will last from it is established. It is, however, important to read the admin rules AND the rules for the server, you child wants admin for. Admin can be taking from the client, if the client miuses it, and attacks the server.
